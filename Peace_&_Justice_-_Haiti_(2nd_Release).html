Artists for Peace and Justice (APJ) do relief work and build schools in Haiti. I have worked with them before to fund-raise right after the Haiti earthquake. My new poster for APJ is to raise funds and awareness for building schools needed in Haiti. Please support this great cause.

We originally released 200 of this print to benefit Artists for Peace and Justice (APJ) with the other 200 to be sold through their channels.
