Prague Spring

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

The Prague Spring was a period of political liberalization in Czechoslovakia that began in early January of 1968 and continued until late August of the same year when the Soviet Union invaded the country to halt the reforms.

A political protest poster from this time, by an unknown artist, serves as the source material for Fairey's Dis Obey and Make Art Not War.
