Orb Audio is the maker of small, stylish, round speakers.

From Orb Audio:

March 20, 2005: Orb Audio as a visual canvas? We were quite flattered when we were approached with the concept of an Orb "art show" featuring the work of prominent and underground artists applied to our classic round speakers. Spanning the spectrum of styles, 20 sets of Orb speakers are being transformed into new works of art by such artists as Haze, Shepard Fairey, Temi, Steve Schwartz and more than a dozen other national and local artists.
