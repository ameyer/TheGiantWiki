This print was not available for purchase on the Obey Giant website. It was only available to those who attended the Supply and Demand retrospective at the Cincinnati Contemporary Arts Center.

From Obeygiant.com:

For those near Cincinnati and interested in seeing Shepard's Supply and Demand, 20 Year Survey, this is the last month it will be on exhibit. The show is scheduled to close at the CAC on August 22nd. To celebrate its final days, we decided to collaborate with the CAC once again with a print. This print is a sister print to the original CAC print released in February. Shepard decided to take a new spin on the print and use the fine art canvas of the Rise Above Control image as the reference...

In addition to the Print, the CAC has launched two FREE interactive phone apps that explore the OBEY/Shepard Fairey Murals around Cincinnati. For more information on the Phone App, please visit http://www.contemporaryartscenter.org/faireytrek.

Going out with a bang, artist Shepard Fairey has created a new exclusive print for the closing of Shepard Fairey: Supply and Demand available only at the CAC!

CAC Members can purchase this exclusive, limited edition (450, signed and numbered), print at the CAC starting Saturday, August 7 at 11am. Purchase limit is 2 per person. Read about it in the Enquirer.

Not a member? If there are prints left, they go on sale to the public August 14. You can join or renew your membership online, by calling 513.345.8422, or in person at the front desk. Don't miss out!
