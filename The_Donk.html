The Donk

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

The Donk is a colloquilism used by Shepard Fairey fans to describe an error and/or timed-out screen encountered when trying to purchase a print via Obeygiant.com.

An example of the conversational use of The Donk is as follows:

ObeyFan1989: "So you showed your dedication and love by F5'ing for 18 hours during which you missed the birth of your daughter and defecated on yourself, plus you have never sold a Shepard Fairey print on Ebay. After all that, did you even end up getting Big Proof Forever?"


ILuvShepardandNeverFlip74520: "After the add button went active, it took an hour to get back from Paypal. When I finally hit 'submit order' all I got was The Donk. After I change my underwear, I'm going to go kick my puppy and vote Republican."

The Donk, autodefecation, puppy kicking, and Glenn Beck are all direct results of Shepard Fairey and Obeygiant.com not appropriately addressing The Horror.
