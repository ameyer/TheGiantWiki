This print is a variant version of Savage Posse. The paper has a discolored look, but it is really frosting on the paper. This design was later used in the 2006 print Giant Savage 2.

From Shepard Fairey:

"I only made 65 of those (note: referring to the Savage Posse prints), but that looks like one that did not get the spray. I only did 10 or 15 to advertise the Solomon show."
