From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Trace, Obey Edition.
Signed Box.


Released during the 2005 San Diego Comic-Con International, this Chub City figurine was designed by Shepard Fairey under the commission of Jada Toys. This exclusive Obey edition of "Trace" was only available on Saturday, July 16th at booth #5313 of the Comic-Con, and was signed and limited to only 1,000 units.
