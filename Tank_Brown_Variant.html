Tank Brown Variant Print

Year: 1996

Part of Set: none
Size: 18x24
Paper: unknown
Print Type: Screen Print
Release Date:

This is a variant of the postal paper variation of the print Tank. Unlike Tank Brown, the flames are not red on this print. The run size of 100 is the combined total for all variations, as the exact split between white and brown paper is unknown. Brown paper versions appear to be much more rare and only one copy of Tank Brown Variant is known to exist. The original rubylith for this print can be viewed here. Tank Brown, Tank Brown Variant and Soldier Star Brown are the only prints known at this time to have been issued on postal paper.
