This print is the first letterpress release by Obey Giant. In addition to being signed and numbered, the print was embossed with an icon face chop; it originally retailed for $60 and was shipped flat. Click for additional images: close-up of dove, back of print, close-up of chop.

From Obeygiant.com:

Letterpress printing is the art of relief printing using a printing press. The PEACE DOVE Letterpress involved locking an etched zinc plate into the bed of a press, inking it, and pressing paper against it to form a lasting impression in the paper. Invented by Johannes Gutenberg circa 1450 AD.
