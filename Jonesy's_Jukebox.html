From ObeyGiant.com:
You know how big of a fan Shepard is of the Sex Pistols... well, they changed his life! Once again Shepard collaborated with Steve Jones to make a screen print of the Jonesy's Jukebox Radio Show Logo, which Shepard created along with the Indie 103 logo. Its a big honor for Shepard to work with Steve and he is happy to show his appreciation. The Jonesy's Jukebox print is signed by both Shepard and Steve Jones, Ed of 300.
