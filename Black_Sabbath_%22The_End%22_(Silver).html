From Obeygiant.com:

BLACK SABBATH "THE END"

I’m very sad that Black Sabbath, one of the greatest bands of all time, is currently on their final tour ever. However, I was very happy when the band asked me to create a tee shirt and poster design for their “The End” tour. I got to see Sabbath with my wife and daughter Vivienne at the Forum in L.A. last week, as always, the band was amazing! I’ve seen Sabbath about a dozen times, and they’ve never been less than awesome. I’m glad my daughter was able to experience them. The Black Sabbath “The End” poster comes out next Tuesday in three different color ways, and this may be the last Obey/Black Sabbath collaboration, so pick up a print if you are a fan of either Black Sabbath or Obey, but ESPECIALLY if you are a fan of Black Sabbath AND Obey! If you are in L.A. don’t miss Black Sabbath at the Hollywood Bowl if you can get a ticket!
– Shepard

The End, Silver Edition. 18 inches by 24 inches screen print on cream Speckle Tone paper. Numbered edition of 325. Signed by Shepard Fairey. $50 each. Avail. Tuesday, Feb. 23 at 10AM PST on ObeyGiant.com in Prints.
