[edit] Paper Types

Before 2000, Obey Giant screenprint paper types varied, but were most often 'pristine white paper'. Around 2000, the paper type was changed to an archival, cream-colored, fibrous paper. Many people feel that this new paper type is not as good as the old white paper because it takes away from the huge contrast that the old prints had.
