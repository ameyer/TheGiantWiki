Giant Subliminal Print

Year: 1995
Run Size: 48
Part of Set: none
Size: 17.5x22.5
Paper: unknown
Print Type: Screen Print
Release Date:

Created for a Shepard Fairey art show held at the Med Deli in Charleston, South Carolina (Shepard's hometown) and was featured in Supply and Demand. The text on the print reads: "giant/subliminal - artworks - opening friday december 22 1995 10:30 pm med deli 90 folly rd charelston sc"

This print was issued prior to Shepard's move to California and represents one of the earliest known Shepard Fairey print works. It is extremely rare, with the only known copy having sold for $2000.00 on eBay.
