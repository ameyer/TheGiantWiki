Leap of Faith Rubylith Fine Art Piece

Name: Leap of Faith Rubylith
Year: 2008
Description: Rubylith stencil
Edition: 1
Size: 10x15
Provenance: White Walls Gallery

This piece retailed for $2100 at The Duality of Humanity. At that show, it was titled "Street Play Rubylith." Subsequently, the name was changed to Leap of Faith for the print release. The image is based off of the work of Martha Cooper.
