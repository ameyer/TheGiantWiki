Slayer Eagle (logo)

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Logo used by the American thrash-metal band Slayer, which made its debut for the band around the time of the release of their 1990 album Seasons in the Abyss.

The Slayer Eagle logo was used as the model for Shepard's 2002 work Slayer Eagle.
