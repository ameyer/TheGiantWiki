From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

[edit] Exhibit

One-of-a-kind painted/altered/etc. 8" Qee toys from 35 different artists. Exhibit was on display in Minneapolis, Los Angeles, Tokyo, and Hong Kong.

[edit] Book

A 96-page book presenting images of custom-designed Qees that were on tour in Minneapolis, Los Angeles, Tokyo, and Hong Kong. The book was sold at ObeyGiant.com in 2005 and was signed by Shepard Fairey.
