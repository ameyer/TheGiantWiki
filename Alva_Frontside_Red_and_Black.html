This print initially retailed for $90 and was available for purchase at the Exhibit A Gallery in Los Angeles. The print was a tie-in to the exhibit "The Detournements of Wynn Miller," which opened on 06/13/2009. On 06/23/2009, the print was released for purchase on Obeygiant.com, for $80. The print is signed by Shepard Fairey, Tony Alva, and Wynn Miller.
