From Obey Giant:
I’m always creating new patterns to use in my street installs and fine art pieces. I used to buy discontinued wallpaper to incorporate patterns into my art, but now I design and print my own. I think these look nice as stand alone prints, not just repeat patterns, so I’m releasing a 100 print edition of eight versions. I’m happy with the blue variations that make sense with the expanded color palette of my more recent art. There will be a few of the full matching numbered sets (all eight prints) available at a lower price per print as well. Check out the details below! -Shepard
