Travis Millard

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Travis Millard is an artist and the creator of Fudge Factroy Comics. Travis and Shepard Fairey collaborated on the 2001 print Hit and Miss.

The print "Hit & Miss" is signed from 2001, but was not sold on the Obey Giant website until February 10th 2002.
