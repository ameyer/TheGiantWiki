Mike Giant

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Mike Giant is an artist who specializes in graffiti and tattoo art. He and Shepard Fairey have collaborated twice, on prints in 2004 and 2005. The print released in 2004, titled "Giant vs. Giant," is horizontally-formatted and was limited to 150 copies. "Giant vs. Giant 2" was released in 2005, and was limited to 450 copies. Both prints are signed by both artists and were sold for $40 ($10 more than a regular print) on the Obey Giant website. The print runs were split 50/50 between Mike Giant and Shepard.
