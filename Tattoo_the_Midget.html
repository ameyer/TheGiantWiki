Tattoo the Midget

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Comparison between the two campaigns.
Shortly after the Obey Giant campaign surfaced, a counter-campaign based on Tattoo the Midget started to emerge. This shortly lived movement claimed "Tattoo the Midget has a bigger posse" and was usually seen along-side of the original Andre the Giant posse sticker.
