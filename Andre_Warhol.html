Andre Warhol Print

Year: 1995
Run Size: 60
Part of Set: none
Size: 17x25
Paper: unknown
Print Type: Screen Print
Release Date:

Created for a screening of Attention Deficit Disorder, described by Jason Filipow as a skateboard video that showcased the small group of skateboarders that Shepard Fairey sponsored via Alternate Graphics.

Per Helen Stickler:

The print was for a joint screening of ADD and Andre the Giant Has a Posse which took place at The Middle East, a club in Cambridge. This is also the first (and only?) depiction of Andre as a woman. I'd been nagging Shep about the lack of female imagery in his art and this was the outcome. He also put this image on a girly t-shirt the same year.
