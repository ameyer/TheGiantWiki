Pretty Vacant Flyers

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Pretty Vacant is a club night in Los Angeles co-promoted by Guerillaone and Obey Giant. It has been housed at both The Spider Club and Nacional in Hollywood.

One of the graphics for this club night later became the print Pretty Vacant.
