Karl Stoecker

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

South Beach Miami-based photographer, most famous for his 1970's glam photography work with Roxy Music. He also routinely did still-life Advertising for YSL, Lancome, and several other well-known retailers. Married to former fashion model and current proprietor of Posh Vintage Patti Stoecker, with whom he has two girls, Sami and Sheena.

Stoecker's 1972 work on Roxy Music's self-titled debut album serves as the basis for Shepard's 2006 Roxy Music Prints.
