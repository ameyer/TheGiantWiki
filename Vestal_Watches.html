Check out this collaboration with VESTAL Watches! Called the MOTORHEAD, this limited fine piece of hardware includes a custom Vestal/OBEY sticker and guitar pick, all enclosed in a very well designed box. The MOTORHEAD watch comes in two different colorways, Silver and Black. Before you decide which you want, we must let you know that the Black version is slight more expensive but has a special laser engraved Star Face/Public Works image on the back. Both watch boxes come signed by Shepard. The MOTORHEAD Black is $200 and the Silver is $175... worth every penny.
