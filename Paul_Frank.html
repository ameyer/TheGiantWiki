Paul Frank

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Paul Frank (born Paul Frank Sunich) is a designer and the creator of Paul Frank Industries, Inc. He and Shepard Fairey have collaborated to create unique clothing accessories, including an Obey Giant wallet, CD wallet, and shoulder bag.
