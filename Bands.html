From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Throughout the years, Shepard Fairey has designed prints for bands, many of which have been sold through obeygiant.com. Whether or not these prints are "Obey Giant" is a topic of debate, because they are not created for the purpose stated in the manifesto, "...reawaken a sense of wonder about one's environment."
