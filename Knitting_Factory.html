Per Helen Stickler:

"I can confirm the print run is definitely 45 [...] All the prints are white paper with silver spray paint, and black screen print. Each one is unique [...]

The poster was created for a screening of my short films, and we had a poster giveaway at the start of the screening. The winner was whoever could answer the trivia question "how did Andre The Giant die?" Only one hand went up, the kid was in the front row and couldn't see that his was the only hand raised in the whole room. The correct answer was of a heart attack, in Paris."
