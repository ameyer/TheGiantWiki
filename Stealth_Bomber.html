Notes:

  • 50 Limited Edition 8" available
  • Designed by a company called BearBearQ, and made by TOY2R for the Qeedrophonic exhibits

  • All toys are signed and numbered

  • Designed by DALEK, CSA DESIGN, OBEY, COOP and HAZE XXL
