From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

In 2005, Shepard Fairey teamed up with Art Official to produce a three-part designer surfboard set.

From Art Official:

Artist Blurb

The OBEY campaign, once a means of getting up while perpetuating a philosophy on the landscape and within minds, has taken little more than a decade to crisscross continents worldwide and adhere onto every surface imaginable.

Artofficial Surfboards in collaboration with Shepard Fairey, continues the OBEY experiment of Phenomenology by wading into a climate that stickers, posters, and stencils must, by necessity, avoid - the ocean.
