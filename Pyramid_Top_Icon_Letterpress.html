I originally created this image as part of a series of canvases I made for the Wooster Collective 10th Anniversary show. I especially liked this one, but didn’t feel it fit with the other three sets I put in the Wooster show. Geometric tension with a minimal number of elements can be striking and elegant. That balance was what I was striving for in this print. If you are a conspiracy theorist, please indulge your most titillating paranoid fantasies about the illuminati sub-text of this image.
