From Obey Giant:
I created this art previously but never released it as a print until now. For this new version, the message reads Knowledge Plus Action Equals Power. The text, "The Future Is Unwritten" was inspired by a quote from Joe Strummer of The Clash. The flower growing from the barbed wire in my art piece is symbolic of the negative being overcome by the positive, a visual metaphor for hope in the unwritten future. – Shepard
