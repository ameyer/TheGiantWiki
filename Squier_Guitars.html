[edit] SQUIER::OBEY Graphic Series

In 2005, Shepard Fairey and Fender guitars teamed up to make a line of guitars.The new OBEY Graphic Series from Squier combines electric guitars with distinctive art and killer vibe to form truly inspirational “freedom of expression” instruments. Features include unique graphic art designs by renowned artist Shepard Fairey, Duncan Designed™ humbucking pickups and special “RusticAndWorn” hardware.
