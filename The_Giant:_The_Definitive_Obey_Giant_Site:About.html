The Giant: The definitive Obey Giant site:About

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

TheGiant.Org was born on January 26th 2006 and was created to be a conglomerate of information about the life and works of Shepard Fairey and his Obey Giant project. The idea was formulated by Obbi and Noverflow where Obbi was responsible for over half of the information included in this database. Noverflow is currently a student at RISD, the same school as the alumni Shepard Fairey, studying industrial design. Please take the time to sit back and enjoy this site and even contribute when you feel you have something to share! We welcome you to TheGiant.Org and hope your time here is well spent.
