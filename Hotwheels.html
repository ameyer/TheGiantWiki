The more things change the more they stay the same. Shepard teamed up with the World Famous Petersen Automotive Museum and Hot Wheels to create this classic mini OBEY VW Bus. He delivered this colorful and compelling design for a modern day hippie van - complete with the message of "PEACE." The detail on this model is amazing! Limited Edition of 5000. Comes signed, once again, by Shepard. For the car collecting, wheat pasting, peace loving Posse Member.
