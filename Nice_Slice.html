Nice Slice

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

A small restaurant on Thayer Street in Providence, RI. This pizza place is filled with Obey Giant artwork, and everything (including the garbage cans) is plastered with Obey Giant stickers. What connection (if any) the shop has with Obey is unknown.

The shop owner, a RISD graduate, seems to know Shepard on a personal level.
