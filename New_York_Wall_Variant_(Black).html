Please note, the only known copy of this print is signed 1997, but the print could have been produced in 1996, alongside the regular run.

Per Helen Stickler:

"This print is oversprayed with glossy black spray paint, not undersprayed with matte metallic silver a'la Knitting Factory. The spray on top of the screen print gives it a bit of iridescence. There is another extraordinary detail: the whites of Andre's eyes are painted in with white-out, making this one of the only early prints that was both hand sprayed and hand painted as well. It is a 2 color print (black and red) on standard white 18x24 paper...

Mine is signed 1997, it's an AP but I know there were more. No idea of the edition."
