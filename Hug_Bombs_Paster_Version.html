Hug Bombs Paster Version
Hug Bombs Paster Version in the streets

Hug Bombs Paster Version was an unsigned, unnumbered offset poster on thin paper. Copies of this poster, along with stickers, were distributed to members of the 'street team' in the run-up to the 2004 presidential election. Shepard also released both screen print and offset lithograph versions of this poster (Hug Bombs and Hug Bombs Offset, respectively).

The text reads, "We need to use our global supremacy to protect our children. We need to use our children to protect our global supremacy."
