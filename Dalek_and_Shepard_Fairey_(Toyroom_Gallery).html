Dalek and Shepard Fairey (Toyroom Gallery)

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site


Joint show that took place at the Toyroom Gallery in Sacremento, California from Friday, August 19, 2005 to Saturday, September 30, 2005. This show was a smaller, stateside follow-up to Dalek and Shepard Fairey (Magda Danysz Gallery).
