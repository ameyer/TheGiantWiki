Matt Goldman
The Studio Number One creative team

Matt Goldman is an artist, designer at Studio Number One, and co-promoter (with Shepard Fairey) of Dance Right, the La Cita Thursday night party where Shepard often spins. On November 28, 2006, Matt's Street Weapons (see below) had the honor of being the first release of the Studio Number One artist series. It sold out in less than an hour.
