From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

In 2001, obeygiant.com sold postcards featuring images of Obey art found in the urban landscape.

From the Obey Giant Limited Edition Street Installation postcard set (700 sets produced): "Each set contains 8 different postcards in a signed and numbered envelope, plus 4 assorted stickers. These cards will not be reprinted. $10.00 per set plus $1.00 shipping."
