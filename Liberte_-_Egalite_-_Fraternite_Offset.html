From Obey Giant:
I created the Liberté, Egalité, Fraternité art piece to show support for France after the Nov. 13, 2015 terror attacks which happened just before the time of my Earth Crisis installation at the Eiffel Tower. The piece was very important to me as a symbol of unity during a dark, tragic moment. I was moved that the art and the message was inspiring for so many people in France, and beyond, and it has remained one of my most requested images since it was first created. I’m excited to be releasing it now as an open edition offset print, accessible for all. I will also be releasing a small number of fine art, large-format letterpress prints of this image. Stay tuned for details. – Shepard
