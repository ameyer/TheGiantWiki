From Obeygiant.com:

Last night was an amazing night. Today is an amazing day. Let’s stay politically engaged and make tomorrow even better. Here are some photos from last night’s AWESOME Obama party in LA. I wheat pasted, Z-Trip DJed, I DJed, Obama won, and my close friends were there to share it. I will be interviewed on CBS News tonight if you are not sick of me already(I’m sick of me). I also made a poster for MoveOn.org that will be out shortly. I think it sums up the unified and cumulative effort that made Obama president. Let’s celebrate today and get to work tomorrow. I’m leaving work to go record shopping.

-Shepard

These 5,000 limited edition prints are 24" x 36" and are offset printed on French Paper Company's Cream Speckletone stock under the artist's supervision. The prints signed by the artist will be available to those who donate $500 or more, while supplies last. For security reasons, we can only ship to credit card billing addresses and only to the United States. Transactions are not confirmed until you receive email confirmation that your payment has been processed.
