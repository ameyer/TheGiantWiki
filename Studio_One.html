Studio Number One Print
the Studio Number One creative team

After leaving BLK/MRKT Shepard Fairey moved on his own and started Studio Number One. On Saturday, September 2, 2006, Jason Filipow, aka The Obey Bachelor, announced on thegiant.org's forum that he was about to become a partial owner of Studio Number One.

"And one more exciting note, I am about to become a partial owner of the famous Studio Number One! I am very excited about that!"

- Jason Filipow, thegiant.org forum
