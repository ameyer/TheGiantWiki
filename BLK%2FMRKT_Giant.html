This print was produced in conjunction with 1997's "BLK/MRKT Group Show"" at the IP Gallery in San Diego, California. This show featured work by Shepard Fairey, Dave Kinsey, and Scott Herskovitz. At least two examples of this print, signed by Shepard Fairey, are known to still exist.
