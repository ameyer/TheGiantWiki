This print was the second collaboration between Mike Giant and Shepard Fairey. The "San Diego" on this print is a reference to the location of the Voice 1156 gallery, site of Giant vs Giant (Voice 1156 Gallery), a joint art show.

Note: This print has been the victim of many unauthorized reproductions. If purchasing this print, make sure the print is signed and numbered.
