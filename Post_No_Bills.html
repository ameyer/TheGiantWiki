Book Cover (hardcover)

A 67-page book, first published in 2002 (hardcover 2005).

"This book, (Post no Bills), is a collection of documented street works from my over a decade running the Obey Giant campaign. The photographs of posters and stencils taken over the past few years are the purest representation of my work aside from one actually stumbling upon it in the urban landscape" - Shepard Fairey.

The 2002 cover of this book was taken from the print Post No Bills Collage. In 2005, a special edition of this book was released by Wooster Collective. This special edition included four mini-prints (Post No Bills Print Set), and sold for around $80.
