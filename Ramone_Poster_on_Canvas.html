Name: Ramone Poster on Canvas
Year: 2002
Description: silkscreen on canvas
Edition: 2 + AP + PP
Size: 18x24
Provenance: gallery

This Ramone Poster on Canvas is unique in being a Printer's Proof (PP). It appears that this piece was a gift, or not-for-sale, as the canvas it was printed on was ripped and repaired with tape prior to silkscreening. In addition, the silkscreening itself was below the usual level of quality associated with fine art prints.
