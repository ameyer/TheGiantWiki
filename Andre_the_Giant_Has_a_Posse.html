Andre the Giant Has a Posse

From The Giant: The Definitive Obey Giant Site

Andre the Giant has a Posse (1997) is a 15-minute documentary, directed by Helen Stickler, about the sticker campaign and the philosophy behind it.
Large portions of this video were used in the video Attention Deficit Disorder.

[edit] Awards

Grand Jury Award for Short Documentary - 1997 Florida Film Festival.
