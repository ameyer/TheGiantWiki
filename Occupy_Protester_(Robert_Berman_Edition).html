This print was only available through Robert Berman Gallery, as part of their Just Occupy gallery show, which ran January 7-28, 2012; it was not sold on Obeygiant.com. The print was released at the opening reception on 01/14/2012. It originally retailed for $99, although 99 prints from the edition were sold as a diptych, paired with a photo, for $2000.
